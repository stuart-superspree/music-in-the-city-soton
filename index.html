#Festival Info
💰
Everything's Free
›

All 361 acts across 54 venues — completely free to attend. No wristband, no ticket. Just walk in.

Most venues are pubs and bars — buying a drink supports the venue, but entry itself is free. Outdoor stages (Arundel Gardens, St Michael's Square, Town Quay Park) are public and free.

📍
Getting Around
›

Old Town cluster (where you are): 94 High St Vault, Canute's Palace, Holyrood, Weigh House, Castle Vault — all within 2-minute walk.

Cultural Quarter: Mettricks Guildhall, The Grumpy Monkey, John Hansard Gallery, Belgium and Blues — 5 min walk north.

Bedford Place (Beards & Boards, Bottle & Stoat, Raven & Bine, Overdraft) — 10 min walk north.

The 1865 / Joiners — in Brunswick Square / St Mary Street, ~8 min walk east.

♿
Accessibility
›

Many venues are historic — some vaults have step access only. John Lewis, HMV, Arundel Gardens and most street venues have step-free access.

Contact MITC via musicinthecity.org/contact-us for specific access needs at any venue.

🎸
About MITC
›

Established in 2008, Music in the City is Southampton's multi-venue community music festival. Celebrating local talent, bringing people together, making live music accessible to all.

Run by Chris Orr since 2024, now evolving into a year-round movement supporting local culture — not just an annual event.

The programme prioritises emerging and grassroots musicians, offering professional stage experience to artists from under-represented backgrounds.

Visit musicinthecity.org →
